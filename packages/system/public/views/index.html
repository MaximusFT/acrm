Introduction

Password management system, built on this site is intended for management of employee access to different operating resources.

It is strongly recommended to keep database up to date.

The system operates in several modes of access levels: administrator, manager, employee, visitor.

  • Administrators have full access to the system and can add, modify, delete any information about users and their passwords.
  • Department managers have access only to the staff of his department and can only change the information about them.
  • Employees only have access to information about themselves.
  • Visitors can only view the news. This is the default access after registering a new user. Such a user must wait until the administrators will not approve him access level employee.
